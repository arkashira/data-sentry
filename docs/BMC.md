# Business Model Canvas – **data‑sentry**

| **Key Partners** | **Key Activities** | **Key Resources** |
|------------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| • Cloud providers (AWS, GCP, Azure) – storage, compute, networking <br>• Encryption & key‑management services (HashiCorp Vault, AWS KMS) <br>• SaaS platform integrators (Auth0, Stripe, HubSpot) <br>• Open‑source backup tools (Restic, Borg) <br>• Compliance auditors (SOC‑2, ISO‑27001) <br>• Reseller & MSP networks | • Develop & maintain multi‑tenant backup & DR engine <br>• Build and publish SDKs / connectors for popular SaaS data sources (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, S3, Snowflake, etc.) <br>• Operate automated backup scheduling, monitoring, and alerting <br>• Run security hardening, encryption, and compliance testing <br>• Provide 24/7 incident response & recovery assistance <br>• Continuous performance & cost optimisation for scale | • Core backup & recovery micro‑service platform (Go/Node) <br>• Multi‑region, encrypted object storage <br>• Scalable orchestration layer (Kubernetes) <br>• Proprietary data‑deduplication & compression algorithms <br>• Customer‑facing SaaS portal (React + API) <br>• Knowledge base & support tooling |
| **Value Proposition** | **Customer Segments** | **Channels** |
|------------------------|----------------------|------------|
| • **Zero‑downtime data protection** – automated, scheduled backups with instant point‑in‑time recovery. <br>• **Enterprise‑grade security** – AES‑256 encryption in‑flight & at‑rest, immutable storage, role‑based access. <br>• **Scalable, pay‑as‑you‑grow** – multi‑tenant architecture that handles terabytes to petabytes without re‑architecting. <br>• **SaaS‑native integration** – pre‑built connectors for common SaaS data stores, API‑first design for custom sources. <br>• **Cost‑avoidance** – reduces churn and revenue loss by preventing data‑loss incidents; pricing model aligns with actual storage used. | • **Fast‑growing SaaS startups** (B2B SaaS, PLG models) that handle user‑generated data and cannot afford downtime. <br>• **Mid‑market SaaS firms** (10‑200 M ARR) needing compliance‑ready DR without large internal ops teams. <br>• **Vertical SaaS** (FinTech, HealthTech, EdTech) with strict data‑privacy regulations. <br>• **Managed Service Providers (MSPs)** that bundle backup/DR for their SaaS clients. | • Direct sales via **product‑led growth** – free tier → upgrade. <br>• Marketplace listings (AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace, GCP Marketplace). <br>• Partner‑driven referrals (cloud consultants, MSPs). <br>• Technical webinars, developer conferences, and open‑source community (SDKs on GitHub). <br>• Content marketing – case studies, whitepapers on data‑loss cost. |
| **Revenue Streams** | **Cost Structure** | **Key Metrics** |
|---------------------|-------------------|----------------|
| • **Subscription SaaS** – tiered plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) based on GB stored & recovery SLA. <br>• **Usage‑based add‑ons** – extra storage, cross‑region replication, accelerated restores. <br>• **Professional services** – migration, custom connector development, compliance audit assistance. <br>• **Marketplace revenue share** – percentage of sales through cloud marketplaces. | • Cloud infrastructure (storage, compute, network egress). <br>• Engineering headcount (core platform, connectors, security). <br>• Security & compliance certifications (SOC‑2, ISO‑27001 audits). <br>• Customer support & SRE on‑call rotation. <br>• Marketing & partner program budgets. <br>• Licensing for third‑party encryption/key‑management tools. | • Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) & ARR growth. <br>• Customer churn & Net Revenue Retention (NRR). <br>• Backup success rate & mean time to recovery (MTTR). <br>• Storage utilization per customer (GB‑month). <br>• Cost per GB stored vs. revenue per GB. <br>• Number of active integrations / SDK downloads. |
| **Unfair Advantage** | **Assumptions & Risks** |
|----------------------|--------------------|
| • **Built‑in multi‑tenant deduplication** reduces storage cost dramatically vs. generic backup tools. <br>• **SaaS‑first design** – API‑first, webhook‑driven, ready for CI/CD pipelines. <br>• **Deep security integration** – immutable object lock, secret‑management, audit logs for compliance‑heavy verticals. | • SaaS customers will prioritize data protection enough to allocate budget early. <br>• Cloud provider pricing remains stable; sudden price hikes could affect margins. <br>• Regulatory changes (e.g., data residency) may require additional infrastructure investments. <br>• Competitive pressure from established DR vendors (Veeam, Rubrik) entering the SaaS niche. |
