# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for data-sentry

## Problem Statement
Data loss and downtime can have devastating consequences for SaaS companies, resulting in significant financial losses, damage to reputation, and user churn. Current backup and disaster recovery solutions often require manual intervention, are prone to human error, and can be costly.

## Target Users
The primary target users for data-sentry are SaaS companies, particularly those with large volumes of sensitive data. These users require a secure, reliable, and automated solution for backup and disaster recovery to ensure business continuity and minimize the risk of data loss.

## Goals
The primary goals of data-sentry are to:

1. **Prevent data loss**: Provide a secure and reliable solution for backup and disaster recovery to prevent data loss and minimize downtime.
2. **Ensure business continuity**: Enable SaaS companies to quickly recover their data in the event of a disaster, minimizing the impact on business operations.
3. **Reduce costs**: Offer a cost-effective solution for data protection, reducing the financial burden of data loss and downtime.
4. **Simplify backup and recovery**: Automate the backup and recovery process, reducing the risk of human error and freeing up valuable resources.

## Key Features (Prioritized)
The following features are critical to achieving the goals of data-sentry:

### Must-Haves

1. **Automated Backups**: Schedule backups to run automatically, ensuring data is always up-to-date.
2. **Disaster Recovery**: Quickly recover data in the event of a disaster, minimizing downtime and data loss.
3. **Data Encryption**: Protect data with industry-standard encryption protocols, both in transit and at rest.
4. **Scalable Architecture**: Handle large volumes of data with ease, ensuring backups are always accessible.

### Nice-to-Haves

1. **Flexible Integration**: Support for a wide range of data sources and destinations, making integration easy.
2. **Real-time Monitoring**: Provide real-time monitoring and alerts for backup and recovery operations.
3. **Customizable Backup Schedules**: Allow users to customize backup schedules to meet their specific needs.
4. **Multi-tenancy**: Support multiple tenants, enabling SaaS companies to manage backups for multiple customers.

## Success Metrics
The following metrics will be used to measure the success of data-sentry:

1. **Backup completion rate**: The percentage of backups completed successfully.
2. **Recovery time objective (RTO)**: The time it takes to recover data in the event of a disaster.
3. **Data loss rate**: The percentage of data lost due to backup failures or other issues.
4. **User satisfaction**: The level of satisfaction among users with the data-sentry solution.

## Scope
The scope of data-sentry includes:

1. **Automated backups**: Schedule backups to run automatically, ensuring data is always up-to-date.
2. **Disaster recovery**: Quickly recover data in the event of a disaster, minimizing downtime and data loss.
3. **Data encryption**: Protect data with industry-standard encryption protocols, both in transit and at rest.
4. **Scalable architecture**: Handle large volumes of data with ease, ensuring backups are always accessible.

## Out-of-Scope
The following features are out-of-scope for data-sentry:

1. **Data analytics**: Provide insights and analytics on data usage and trends.
2. **Data warehousing**: Store and manage large datasets for reporting and analytics.
3. **Machine learning**: Use machine learning algorithms to predict and prevent data loss.
4. **Integration with third-party services**: Integrate with third-party services, such as CRM or ERP systems.
