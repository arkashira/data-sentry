# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
Data Sentry is an automated backup and disaster recovery solution designed specifically for SaaS companies, ensuring data integrity and minimizing user churn.

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**  
1. Data Sentry: Your safety net for automated backups, protecting your SaaS business from data loss.
2. Safeguard your SaaS with Data Sentry—automated disaster recovery that keeps your data secure and your users happy.
3. Data Sentry: The essential tool for SaaS companies to automate backups and recover from disasters effortlessly.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Sarah, SaaS Operations Manager**  
   - **Daily Job:** Overseeing daily operations, ensuring data integrity, and managing backup processes.  
   - **Budget:** $5,000/month for operational tools.

2. **Persona 2: Tom, CTO of a Mid-sized SaaS Company**  
   - **Daily Job:** Responsible for technology strategy, infrastructure management, and data security.  
   - **Budget:** $10,000/month for tech solutions.

3. **Persona 3: Lisa, Product Manager at a Startup**  
   - **Daily Job:** Managing product features, user feedback, and ensuring seamless user experience.  
   - **Budget:** $3,000/month for product management tools.
