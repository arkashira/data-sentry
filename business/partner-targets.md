## Partner Integration Roadmap for Data-Sentry

### Target Integrations

| Partner/API               | Free-Tier Limits                     | Integration Effort | Value-Add Job to Solve                                      | Rationale                                                                 |
|---------------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **AWS S3**                | 5 GB of standard storage per month   | M                  | Secure and scalable storage for backups                      | Widely used by SaaS companies for storage; potential for revenue-share.   |
| **Google Cloud Storage**  | 5 GB of storage per month            | M                  | Automated backup and retrieval of data                       | Popular among SaaS for cloud storage; affiliate programs available.       |
| **Dropbox**               | 2 GB of storage                       | S                  | Easy file sharing and collaboration for backup files        | High user base; potential for affiliate revenue.                          |
| **Microsoft Azure Blob**  | 5 GB of storage per month            | M                  | Reliable storage for backup solutions                        | Strong presence in enterprise SaaS; revenue-share opportunities.          |
| **Zapier**                | 100 tasks per month                  | S                  | Automate workflows for backup triggers                       | Connects with numerous SaaS apps; affiliate program available.            |
| **Slack**                 | Free tier available                   | S                  | Notifications and alerts for backup status                   | High engagement platform; potential for increased user retention.         |
| **Stripe**                | No free tier, but low transaction fees| L                  | Payment processing for subscription-based backup services     | Revenue-share potential; aligns with SaaS business models.                |
| **Twilio**                | 5,000 SMS messages per month          | M                  | SMS alerts for backup failures                               | Enhances user engagement; potential for affiliate revenue.                |

### Summary
The integrations listed above target key SaaS platforms that are commonly used by companies needing automated backup and disaster recovery solutions. The focus is on partnerships that not only enhance the functionality of Data-Sentry but also provide opportunities for revenue-sharing and affiliate programs, ensuring a mutually beneficial relationship. Prioritizing integrations with manageable effort and significant user value will facilitate a smoother rollout and adoption of Data-Sentry in the market.
