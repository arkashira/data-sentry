# Business Model Canvas for Data-Sentry

## Customer Segments
- **SaaS Companies**: Small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) looking to automate their backup processes.
- **IT Managers**: Professionals responsible for data integrity and disaster recovery in organizations.
- **Compliance Officers**: Individuals ensuring that companies meet data protection regulations.
- **Startups**: New businesses that require cost-effective and reliable backup solutions.

## Value Propositions
- **Automated Backups**: Seamless, scheduled backups that reduce manual intervention and human error.
- **Disaster Recovery**: Quick restoration of data to minimize downtime and user churn.
- **Cost Efficiency**: Competitive pricing model starting at $29/month (~1,000 THB) for small businesses.
- **Compliance Assurance**: Helps businesses meet regulatory requirements for data protection.

## Channels
- **Website**: Direct sales through a user-friendly platform with detailed product information.
- **SaaS Marketplaces**: Listing on platforms like G2, Capterra, and AWS Marketplace.
- **Social Media**: Targeted ads on LinkedIn and Facebook to reach IT professionals and decision-makers.
- **Webinars**: Educational sessions demonstrating the product’s capabilities and benefits.

## Customer Relationships
- **Self-Service**: Comprehensive documentation and FAQs for users to troubleshoot independently.
- **Customer Support**: 24/7 chat support for urgent issues and personalized assistance.
- **Community Forums**: User-driven forums for sharing experiences and solutions.
- **Onboarding Assistance**: Guided setup and integration support for new customers.

## Revenue Streams
- **Subscription Model**: Monthly or annual subscription fees starting at $29/month (~1,000 THB).
- **Tiered Pricing**: Different pricing tiers based on storage needs and features (e.g., $49/month for advanced features).
- **Add-On Services**: Additional charges for premium support or custom backup solutions.
- **Affiliate Partnerships**: Revenue from partners who refer customers to Data-Sentry.

## Key Resources
- **Development Team**: Skilled engineers for product development and maintenance.
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: Reliable cloud services for data storage and backup solutions.
- **Customer Support Team**: Trained personnel to assist users and resolve issues.
- **Marketing Resources**: Tools and platforms for promoting the product effectively.

## Key Activities
- **Product Development**: Continuous improvement and feature updates based on user feedback.
- **Marketing Campaigns**: Running targeted campaigns to acquire new customers.
- **Customer Support**: Providing timely assistance and maintaining customer satisfaction.
- **Partnership Development**: Building relationships with other SaaS providers for integrations.

## Key Partners
- **Cloud Service Providers**: Partnerships with AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure for reliable storage solutions.
- **SaaS Marketplaces**: Collaborations with platforms that list SaaS products for increased visibility.
- **Compliance Organizations**: Partnerships with firms that specialize in data protection regulations.
- **Affiliate Marketers**: Engaging influencers and bloggers in the tech space to promote Data-Sentry.

## Cost Structure
- **Development Costs**: Salaries for the development team and software tools (~$15,000/month).
- **Marketing Expenses**: Budget for online ads, content creation, and promotional activities (~$5,000/month).
- **Cloud Storage Fees**: Costs associated with data storage and backup services (~$2,000/month).
- **Customer Support Costs**: Salaries and tools for the support team (~$3,000/month).
