# 🚀 data-sentry
**Power SaaS companies with automated backup and disaster recovery solutions to prevent data loss and user churn.** data-sentry is an innovative solution designed to provide a secure and reliable way to protect sensitive data and ensure business continuity.

## Why data-sentry?
- **Scalable**: Built to handle large volumes of data, ensuring that your backups are always up-to-date and easily accessible.
- **Secure**: Utilizes industry-standard encryption protocols to safeguard your data, both in transit and at rest.
- **Reliable**: Designed with redundancy in mind, ensuring that your data is always available when you need it.
- **Flexible**: Supports a wide range of data sources and destinations, making it easy to integrate with your existing infrastructure.
- **Automated**: Simplifies the backup and recovery process, reducing the risk of human error and freeing up valuable resources.
- **Cost-effective**: Offers a cost-effective solution for data protection, reducing the financial burden of data loss and downtime.
- **Built for SaaS**: Specifically designed to meet the unique needs of SaaS companies, providing a tailored solution for data protection and disaster recovery.

## Feature Overview
| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Automated Backups | Schedule backups to run automatically, ensuring your data is always up-to-date. |
| Disaster Recovery | Quickly recover your data in the event of a disaster, minimizing downtime and data loss. |
| Data Encryption | Protect your data with industry-standard encryption protocols, both in transit and at rest. |
| Scalable Architecture | Handle large volumes of data with ease, ensuring your backups are always accessible. |
| Flexible Integration | Support for a wide range of data sources and destinations, making integration easy. |
